3 Interesting Facts about Pandas!

  1. Pandas are quite lazy animals! They spend most of their day eating and sleeping
  2. Pandas are good at climbing trees, and they can swim!
  3. Pandas in the wild have a life span of about 20 years

3 Interesting Facts about Chameleons!

  1. Chameleons have eyes on the back of their heads!
  2. Unlike most lizards, Chameleons can't grow their tails back
  3. Chameleons will sometimes change color based on their mood
